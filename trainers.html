Meet Our Personal Trainers

Dave Bradford

If your goal is to get stronger:

Dave has over 20 years experience in the body building industry. If you are just starting your body building journey or need a bit of extra help to get you across the line, Dave is your guy. A qualified nutritionist, Dave will discuss with you the right plan for you.

Scott Lacey

If your goal is to lose weight:

Scott is a qualified personal trainer and nutritionist with over 5 years of experience. Having lost over 30kg himself, he knows first hand the difficulties of this journey. Scott will tailor a program that will give you optimum results and get you to your goal.

Jenny Cleary

If your goal is to run a marathon:

Jenny's journey started 3 years ago when she completed Couch to 5k, and since then has gone on to complete 7 marathons. Whatever your reason for wanting to do a marathon, Jenny will show you that anyone can cross the finish line, you just start with putting one foot forward.

Louise Bassford

If your goal is to tone up:

Louise knows all about toning up that body into the desired sculpture. If you have excess fat to burn or just want to get some definition to your body, Louise is the trainer for you. Louise has over 12 years personal training experience and has a qualification in Advanced Nutrition.
